Frequently Asked Questions

What is your response time? expand_more

We typically respond to inquiries within 24 hours during business hours. Urgent matters are addressed within 2-4 hours.

Can I schedule a test drive online? expand_more

Yes! You can schedule a test drive through our inventory page or by contacting our sales department. We offer flexible scheduling to fit your availability.

Do you accept trade-ins? expand_more

Absolutely! We offer competitive trade-in valuations. Bring your vehicle for a free evaluation, or provide details online for an instant quote.

What financing options do you offer? expand_more

We offer flexible financing with competitive rates starting from 2.9% APR. Terms range from 12 to 84 months with various down payment options.

How often should I service my vehicle? expand_more

We recommend servicing every 6 months or 10,000 km, whichever comes first. Our service plans are flexible and can be tailored to your vehicle's needs.
